## ⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING

Bio-fuel production involves fire, explosion, and chemical hazards.

**DO NOT attempt production unless you have:**
- Professional industrial/commercial facility
- Minimum two trained operators
- Fire suppression systems
- Required permits and insurance
- Regulatory compliance

**Residential production is absolutely prohibited and extremely dangerous.**
